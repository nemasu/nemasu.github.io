Movement Guide

Chin Tuck & Scapular Squeeze

Draw your chin straight back to create a "double chin" (don't look down). Simultaneously pinch your shoulder blades together like you're squeezing a pencil between them. Keep shoulders away from ears.

Wall Slides (Cactus Position)

Stand with heels, butt, upper back, and back of head touching the wall. Raise arms into "cactus" position (elbows and back of hands touching the wall). Slowly slide arms up and down a few inches while keeping all points in contact with the wall.

The Nerve Glide

Extend one arm out, palm up. Tilt head AWAY from the arm while curling wrist IN. Then tilt head TOWARD the arm while extending wrist OUT. Do 5–10 times slowly twice daily (no timer).
